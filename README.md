# Superstore-KPI-Dashboard-using-Excel
This project presents a Sales KPI Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using the Superstore dataset. The dashboard provides an overview of sales performance, regional performance, category performance, and top-selling products through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.
## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales data and identify trends, high-performing categories, top-selling products, and regional performance to support data-driven business decisions.
## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Data Visualization

## Dataset

Superstore Sales Dataset
## Dashboard Features

### Yearly Sales Trend
Tracks sales performance from 2014 to 2017 to identify growth patterns and business trends.

### Sales per Category
Compares sales performance across:
- Furniture
- Office Supplies
- Technology

### Total Sales per Region
Analyzes revenue generated by:
- Central
- East
- South
- West

### Top 10 Most-Sold Products
Highlights products contributing the highest revenue.

### Interactive Filters
Users can filter dashboard results by region using slicers.
## Key Insights

- Sales showed an overall positive trend from 2014 to 2017.
- Technology generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
- The West region recorded the strongest sales performance.
- The Central region generated the lowest sales among all regions.
- A small number of high-value products contributed significantly to overall revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Technology products due to strong customer demand.
- Analyze successful sales strategies in the West region and apply them to lower-performing regions.
- Develop targeted marketing campaigns for the Central region to improve sales performance.
- Prioritize inventory planning for top-performing products to maximize revenue opportunities.
